# Future Features

Exciting new features planned for the Anti-Diabetes AI App.

## 1. Advanced AI Coaching
- **Deep Personalization**: AI that learns from your habits and provides better advice over time.
- **Contextual Awareness**: Suggestions based on your current location (e.g., "There's a healthy restaurant nearby").
- **Predictive Analytics**: Predicting potential glucose spikes based on past data and planned meals.

## 2. Community & Social
- **Support Groups**: Connect with other users managing diabetes.
- **Challenges**: Gamified health goals (e.g., "10,000 steps for 7 days").
- **Peer Mentoring**: Connect experienced users with those new to diabetes management.

## 3. Device Integration
- **CGM Support**: Direct sync with Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGM) like Dexcom or Libre.
- **Wearables**: Syncing steps and heart rate data from Apple Watch, Fitbit, and Garmin.

## 4. Content Library
- **Recipe Database**: Diabetes-friendly recipes with automatic carb counting.
- **Educational Videos**: Short clips on exercise, diet, and mental health.
- **Expert Articles**: Verified information from endocrinologists and nutritionists.

## 5. Localization
- Support for multiple languages to make the app accessible globally.
